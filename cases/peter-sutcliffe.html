Peter Sutcliffe: The Yorkshire Ripper

A forensic dive into one of Britain's most notorious serial killers

Between 1975 and 1980, Peter Sutcliffe murdered 13 women and attempted to kill 7 others across northern England. Dubbed the Yorkshire Ripper, his crimes sparked one of the largest manhunts in British history. Sutcliffe claimed he heard voices commanding him to kill, but forensic evidence and psychological evaluations revealed a far more calculated pattern.
