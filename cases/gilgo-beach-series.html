THE GILGO BEACH SERIES INDEX

A guide to the documentaries covering the **Rex Heuermann / LISK** case.

The Architect's Double Life

The arrest of Rex Heuermann in 2023 ended a decade-long search for the Long Island Serial Killer (LISK). These two major series—one focused on the victims and the other on the shocking investigation—provide a complete picture of the case, from the victims' disappearances to the final DNA match.

House of Secrets (3 Episodes)

Focuses primarily on the investigation and the shocking reveal of the successful architect suspect.

  • E01: The Architect's Double Life
  • E02: A Decade of Silence
  • E03: The Pizza Crust Alibi

Lost Girls (3 Episodes)

Based on the non-fiction book, this series centers on the victims, particularly Mari Gilbert's desperate search for her daughter, Shannan.

  • E01: The Desperate Search for Shannan
  • E02: Finding the Gilgo Four
  • E03: The Forgotten Victims
